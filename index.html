How we get from here to there

A realistic pathway to peace — built on incremental trust, economic cooperation, and shared governance of sacred spaces.

Mutual Recognition & Framework

Israel and Palestine formally recognize each other's sovereignty. International community provides security guarantees and economic development packages.

🏗️

Infrastructure & Economic Corridors

The Wider Vision

An interconnected Middle East:
From Abraham Accords to economic powerhouse

Israeli-Palestinian peace is the missing piece that transforms the Abraham Accords from bilateral deals into a regional economic revolution. A peaceful Palestine unlocks Saudi normalization, full GCC integration, and a $1 trillion Middle Eastern common market.

The Riyadh–Tel Aviv Corridor

Saudi normalization — contingent on Palestinian statehood — opens a $800B bilateral trade corridor. High-speed rail through Jordan connects the region's two largest economies in under 4 hours.

Innovation Without Borders

Israeli AI and cybersecurity meets Gulf capital and scale. Joint tech accelerators in Dubai, Riyadh, and Ramallah create the world's newest innovation triangle — the "Silicon Crescent."

The Red Sea Riviera

Saudi Arabia's NEOM and Israel's Eilat merge into a joint tourism mega-zone. Visa-free travel across the Gulf of Aqaba creates a $30B annual tourism corridor with Jordan and Egypt.
